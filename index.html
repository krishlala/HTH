Why donate?

By donating today, you will give hope to a family in need. As of 2020, over 580,466 people are homeless in the U.S. For those 580,466, there are only 291,837 beds in emergency shelters across the U.S. Only around half of those homeless get beds. 50.2%. The rest sleep on the streets, under bridges, and in even worse conditions. The Covid-19 situation has caused homelessness to rise, less beds for the homeless and it is harder to earn the money they need. By donating today, you give a person hope.
